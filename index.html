Gear up for the NXT!

The world looks very different than it did at the start of 2020. More businesses have now embraced the digital-first world.

With increased digital adoption, customer expectations are on the rise. As the goalposts keep shifting, business leaders need to be thinking of what’s NXT.

At Freshworks Experience NXT, you’ll hear from the experts on what you need to thrive in the experience economy, and how you can enhance your CX, accelerate your sales and take your IT into the future!

Drop by for some great conversations, actionable insights and networking opportunities.

Why Experience NXT?

Experience NXT brings together experts, across Customer Experience, Employee Experience, Sales and Marketing in a series of insightful sessions that will help you win customers for life.

You will get to:

  • Learn, interact and network with thought leaders, CEOs, CIOs, VPs and Customer Champions
  • Attend masterclass sessions with our in-house experts
  • Network with industry peers.
